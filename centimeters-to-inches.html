About Centimeters

Centimeters are part of the metric system and are commonly used for product sizes, packaging, garment measurements, and printed dimensions.

They are especially useful when you want a measurement that is more precise than meters but still easy to read in specifications, shopping guides, and manufacturing documents.

About Inches

Inches are an imperial unit often used in the United States for screens, furniture, hardware, consumer products, and room details.

You will often see inches in device sizes, monitor descriptions, construction references, and product listings aimed at US-based buyers or teams.
