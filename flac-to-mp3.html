About FLAC

FLAC is a lossless audio format designed to keep the original signal while reducing file size compared with raw uncompressed audio.

It is great for archiving and hi-fi listening, but the files are larger than MP3 and not every lightweight player or platform handles FLAC equally well.

About MP3

MP3 is the most familiar compressed audio format for web playback, downloads, podcasts, email attachments, and quick sharing.

Its broad device support makes MP3 a dependable target when you want smaller files that open easily across phones, browsers, cars, and older apps.
