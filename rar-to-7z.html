About RAR to 7Z

🆓 This RAR to 7Z converter is a free online converter for repacking RAR archives into 7Z files directly in the browser. Use it when you need a different archive format for sharing, long-term storage, or compatibility with tools that prefer 7Z over RAR. The page supports multiple archives in one run, so it also works well for quick batch archive conversion.

🔒 The workflow is designed for local processing with WebAssembly. Your RAR files stay on your device, extraction happens in your browser, and the new 7Z archive is built on your device. Under normal use, this online RAR to 7Z converter does not upload files to a remote server, which makes it a more privacy-friendly option for internal bundles, client packages, and sensitive documents.

⚙️ Unlike a basic one-click archive tool, this page also lets you enter an optional password for encrypted RAR sources and pick a compression level that matches your priority. That makes the converter practical for both fast repacking jobs and smaller final 7Z outputs.

About RAR

RAR is a proprietary archive format known for efficient compression, recovery features, and support for split archives. It is common in downloaded software packages, backup sets, and file-sharing workflows. Some RAR archives are password-protected, which is why this page includes an optional password field before extraction starts.

RAR is widely recognized, but not every environment has built-in support for creating or managing RAR files. Converting RAR to 7Z can make the archive easier to standardize across mixed teams and toolchains.

About 7Z

7Z is a modern archive format associated with strong compression ratios and flexible compression methods. Many people choose 7Z when they want smaller archive sizes, especially for large folders or collections of documents, code, and media assets.

A 7Z archive is also a practical choice when you want a single portable package that is easy to store and distribute. This RAR to 7Z converter helps you move from an incoming RAR package to a more efficient 7Z output without leaving the browser.

FAQ

What does a RAR to 7Z converter do?

It opens the source RAR archive, extracts the contents locally, and then repacks those files into a new 7Z archive for download.

Is this RAR to 7Z converter free and online?

Yes. This is a free online converter that runs in the browser, so you can convert RAR to 7Z without installing a desktop archiver first.

Does the converter upload my archive?

No. The page is built for local browser processing with WebAssembly, so your archive stays on your device during normal conversion.

When should I use Store, Fast, or Best compression?

Use Store when speed matters most, Fast for a balanced everyday setting, and Best when you want the smallest 7Z output and do not mind higher CPU and memory use.

Can I convert a password-protected RAR file?

Yes, as long as you know the password. Enter it in the Password field so the converter can open the source archive before building the new 7Z file.

Can I convert multiple RAR archives at once?

Yes. Choose multiple RAR files, run the converter once, and then download each 7Z result separately after processing finishes.
